Recovery & Injury Prevention

RECOVER
SMARTER

Recovery is where growth happens. Reduce injury risk, restore mobility, and come back stronger every session.

🔥

WARM-UP SCIENCE

Never skip your warm-up. It increases blood flow, raises muscle temperature, and reduces injury risk by up to 50%.

🦵 5 min light jog or jumping jacks
🔄 Hip circles — 10 each direction
🙆 Arm swings and shoulder rolls
🧎 Leg swings — 10 each leg
⬆️ Inch worms — 5 reps
❄️

COOL-DOWN PROTOCOL

Cooling down gradually lowers heart rate, removes lactic acid, and improves next-day soreness. Takes only 10 minutes.

🚶 5 min slow walk or light jog
🧘 Forward fold hold — 30 sec
💺 Seated hamstring stretch — 30 sec each
🦷 Hip flexor lunge stretch — 30 sec each
😮‍💨 Deep diaphragmatic breathing — 2 min
⚠️

COMMON MISTAKES

Avoid these training errors that sideline athletes every week. Knowledge is your best injury prevention.

❌ Skipping warm-up to save time
❌ Training same muscle groups daily
❌ Ignoring minor pain signals
❌ Poor posture during heavy lifts
❌ Not sleeping 7–9 hours consistently
🧘‍♂️

MOBILITY DRILLS

Improve joint health and athletic range of motion with these daily mobility essentials.

🔄 90/90 hip stretch — 45 sec each
🐈 Cat-cow spine mobilization — 10 reps
🦅 Thoracic rotation — 10 reps each
⬇️ Deep squat hold — 60 seconds
💪 Shoulder capsule stretch — 30 sec
💧

HYDRATION GUIDE

Even 2% dehydration drops athletic performance significantly. Hydration is not optional — it's performance fuel.

🕗 500ml water upon waking up
⚽ 400–600ml 90 min before training
💦 150–250ml every 15–20 min during
📊 500ml per 0.5kg lost during training
🍌 Electrolytes for sessions over 60 min
😴

SLEEP FOR ATHLETES

Growth hormone peaks during deep sleep. Quality sleep is the most underutilized performance enhancer in existence.

⏰ Aim for 8–10 hours nightly
🌡️ Cool dark room below 19°C
📵 No screens 60 min before bed
⏰ Same sleep/wake time every day
😪 10–20 min naps boost afternoon focus
